UI Design

In early 2020, Carepay’s design system was still in its infancy. Based on Brad Frost’s atomic design principles, color schemes and typefaces were defined. A complete set of atoms were designed which were the bases of the molecules, organisms, templates, and pages. This not only improved the consistency of the designs but also eased the communications between the design team and the front-end team by offering agreed guidelines on the design.

02Color palette

Our color palette is based on WCAG 2.0 (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines). Furthermore, it is optimized to better support the target market, the healthcare industry in Africa using smaller screens with low resolution. We considered the 256-color screen and came up with a set of greyscales that is compatible.

04Grid system
