ONETIME


How can we intuitively show different timezones?

The concept introduces a new world clock that shows your favorite time zones on one simple dial.


Internal Project / Lunar Europe / 2014

Christian Moser / Jan König / Suhwa Lee

CONTRIBUTION

Concept development and UI/UX Design, after given with a design brief: to make a world clock on one dial

  • # INTERACTION DESIGN
  • # DIGITAL DESIGN
  • # GRAPHIC DESIGN

CONCEPT

More and more we are all living and working globally. So we need a way to simply see the time anywhere at a glance. That’s how Onetime™ was born. Say goodbye to that wall full of clocks and forget that mental math we’ve been doing. Say hello to Onetime™ today.

“Time travel™”.
With a simple swipe, users can quickly find the right moment to schedule that important teleconference or catch the loved ones before their bedtimes. Beautifully embedded elements show not only the time, but the date change as well.
