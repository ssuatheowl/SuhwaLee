UI design

During the peak days, almost 1000 agents per day in Kenya and Nigeria are using the Carepay Agent app to enroll new beneficiaries across the country, especially to reach out to the poor living in villages. While the app has been up and running for 2 years, no feedback was gathered nor any UX improvement was made. So this was a perfect opportunity to hear the agentsâ€™ voices and make it a ritual to improve the app.

02Remote moderated app testing

I believe that concept validation is an important key to create a successful product and service that meets the user needs. So I have also done various validations by conducting a series of usability testing in my career. And COVID-19 pandemic changed my work situation, working from home connecting with colleagues digitally, to begin with. And Usability testing had to adapt as well. The conventional way is challenged because I simply couldn't fly to Africa, meet the user, and hand over a test device in a laboratory setup.

So I came up with a setup that can enable me and the team to talk to users of Kenya and Nigeria.

03Users in the Heart

On the top of user research and usability testing, various methods were applied to understand the users and to evaluate the product better quantitatively.
System Usability Scale was used to evaluate the product usability quantitatively. Satisfaction score was collected to measure the user satisfaction more objectively.

04Making It A Ritual

To promote the ritual of objectively measuring user satisfaction and product usability, we created a page to show the results at glance. The page will be filled with evaluation results of all products of our platform.

05Design

To help the less tech-savvy agents using the app, brief onboarding tutorials were added.

The home page was restructured to show the statuses of registrations at glance. Throughout the registration process, simple and clear guidelines were added to reduce human errors when filling out a lengthy registration form for a health scheme.

06Results

The user satisfaction has been increased by 12.75% after the redesign. The redesign reduced the number of support calls coming from field agents experiencing an issue with the app. The training time and budget of the agents have been known to be reduced as well.
