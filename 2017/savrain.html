SAVRAIN

EMW (Electronic Magnetic Wave) level indicator encouraging people to consume less electricity by showing the harmful effects of the EMW.

Product Design Program Coursework / KAIST / 2011

Heeyun Koo / Heejeong Heo / Suhwa Lee

CONTRIBUTION

User Research / Concept generation / took a lead in creating user scenarios, graphics, and the package design

  • # PRODUCT DESIGN
  • # DESIGN FOR SUSTAINABILITY
  • # NUDGE DESIGN

CONCEPT

The objective of the project was to design a product that encourages students to save energy. My team decided to persuade KAIST students to save electricity in dormitories where most students do not care at all about their energy consumption.

Savrain is a composition of two words, Save and Brain. It basically detects the amount of EMW(Electro magnetic Waves), and shows the level of the waves in the form of a brain gauge in Savrain. Savrain informs the harmful of EMW as well as the level it. The use of brain is inspired from one of the harmful effects of EMW, bad influence on a brain. Being aware of those information, the user will try to reduce the usage of electronic appliances not because they want to save energy, but because they want to keep themselves healthy and smart by reducing the amount of EMW, and that is the core aspect of Savrain. It does not force people to care about saving energy. Using natural behaviour of people to keep themselves healthy, it naturally leads people to save electricity.
