TAMAGOTCHI ME

Tamagotchi made into a Flash game reflecting myself as a designer

Reflection on Designing Coursework/ TU Delft / 2013

CONTRIBUTION

Individual project, Idea generation, graphics, and Flash/Actionscript 2.0 prototyping

  • # INTERACTION DESIGN
  • # GRAPHIC DESIGN
  • # GAMIFICATION

CONCEPT

As a part of Reflection on Designing course from TU Delft, an assignment to describe myself as a designer was given, in any medium. And I chose a game, specifically in Tamagotchi form to deliver an essay about who I am as a designer.

WHY DAMAGOTCHI?
I think Tamagotchi is a good metaphor to show my passion for interactivity, interest in games, my cultural background at the same time. I am very interested in interaction design and games. And along with my cultural background being raised up in Korea playing with lots of Tamagotchi during my childhood made me come up with the idea of adopting Tamagotchi concept to express myself. Tamagotchi also seemed like a good metaphor of explaining me, since it is about growing a character in the game.




PLAY THE GAME

What kind of things my character will dislike and reject to deal with?
What else would make the character happy, shocked, stressed, or even shocked?

Can you make the character to be a designer?
What tragedy is waiting if you fail to see the designer ending?
