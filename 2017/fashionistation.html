FASHIONISTATION


How can we benefit various stakeholders of Dongdaemun fashion market and promote the market itself?

The final design introduces an interactive screen installation to connect visitors/merchants/fashion designers to share their styles, tips and information in Dongdaemun fashion market.


GoGlobal 2012 / KAIST-RCA / 2012

Ting-han Chen / Sangwoo Park / Soomin Yang / Heejeong Heo / Suhwa Lee

CONTRIBUTION

Research process, concept generation, system architecture and on-screen interaction design for the screen installation

  • # INTERACTION DESIGN
  • # SYSTEM DESIGN
  • # INSTALLATION DESIGN

CONCEPT

The project was a collaboration project between ID KAIST and RCA IDE. The objectives were to design a system for Dongdaemun market which is the number one fashion market in Korea. The theme was to enhance human social connection in the market.

Our team designed ‘Fashionistation’ system which could enhance social connection between stakeholders of Dongdaemun market.
Fashionistation which is installed in the main streets of Dongdaemun market provides unique experience of communication between all the visitors of the market and fashion critics, local merchants. Style pictures taken in Fashionistation photo booth could be shared with other visitors. Interactive display enables people to find and compare fashions which were uploaded by themselves, other visitors and merchants. Visitors of the market could see others’ fashion styles and make a comment on them. World famous designers also can make a critic about participants’ fashion.
