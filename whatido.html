I AM A DESIGNER,
A RESEARCHER
& A MAKER.





I work as a UX designer.

I am a design researcher.

I am an enthusiastic maker, too!

I am a passionate designer who puts the users in the center of focus. Through my professional career, I learned to come up with solutions that balances the user needs, business wishes and technical requirements. I am also capable of UI graphic design and have a keen eye for detail.

I always try to find 'why's before designing to understand the users. I think about values, motives and concerns before screen interactions. I fulfill the curiosity by reading data, papers, taking cultural interviews, and conducting user tests. and I design to meet the user needs, what users really want.

I enjoy making things alive; not only digital, but also inclding tangible ones. My skills in tangible interaction made this possible. I believe in the future where we finally step up with our screen game, and everything will come alive and becomes more tangible. With a better understanding of various technologies, I learn to communicate better with engineers/developers.
